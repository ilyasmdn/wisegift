Frequently Asked Questions

What is WiseGift?

WiseGift is an AI-powered platform that helps you find the perfect gift for any occasion based on the recipient's interests, age, and more. Our advanced AI technology generates personalized gift ideas tailored to your specific requirements.

How does the gift recommendation work?

We use advanced AI technology to analyze the information you provide and generate personalized gift suggestions tailored to your recipient's preferences, interests, and the occasion.

Is WiseGift free to use?

Yes, WiseGift is completely free to use for generating gift suggestions.

Can I buy gifts directly from WiseGift?

No, we provide gift suggestions and in future updates, links to where you can purchase them from other websites.
